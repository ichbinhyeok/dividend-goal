What This Site Focuses On

Money First is dedicated to dividend math translation. It does not provide investment advice, stock picks, or market predictions.

My goal is to take public, static yield data and translate it into a tangible "lifestyle cost" context. Instead of just seeing a percentage, you see what that percentage means for your monthly bills. This helps bridge the gap between abstract financial metrics and real-world goals.

Data Transparency & Privacy

Trust is earned by NOT tracking you. Here is exactly how this site works:

  • No User Tracking: I do not use tools to record your session or map your behavior.
  • No Personal Data: This calculator has no database for user inputs. Nothing you type is saved or sent to a third party.
  • Server-Side Math: All calculations happen instantly on the server using a static dataset. This means the site is fast, lightweight, and completely stateless.

Who This Tool Is For

This tool is designed for U.S.-based users who are in the educational phase of their financial journey.

  • It IS for you if you want to understand the mechanics of dividend yields and goal setting.
  • It IS NOT for you if you are looking for real-time trading data or professional investment recommendations.

Please consult with a certified financial planner or fiduciary before making any investment decisions. Financial planning is personal, and this tool only provides general mathematical models.
