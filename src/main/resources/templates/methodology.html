Calculation Methodology

1. Data Sources

We aggregate market data from Seeking Alpha, Yahoo Finance, and official SEC filings (10-K). Data is reviewed and updated periodically to reflect current market conditions.

Data Sources

We aggregate market data from reputable financial providers to ensure our analysis is current. Our primary data points (Yield, Price, Payout Ratio) are sourced from:

  • Yahoo Finance API (Real-time pricing)
  • Seeking Alpha (Dividend Consistency Records)
  • Nasdaq (Historical Dividend Data)

Data is refreshed daily. However, yields fluctuate with stock price changes in real-time.

Data Currency & Integrity

Dataset Version: 2026-01

We prioritize accuracy over coverage. If a stock's official yield or payout data is unavailable from our primary sources, we do NOT generate estimated values. In such cases, the calculator will display "Data Unavailable".

Risk Assessment

Risk levels (Low, Medium, High) are determined by:

  • Dividend History (Years of growth)
  • Payout Ratio (Safe is under 60% for standard stocks, 90% for REITs)
  • Volatility (Beta)
