Calculation Methodology

1. Data Sources

We aggregate market data from Seeking Alpha, Yahoo Finance, and official SEC filings (10-K). Data is updated daily.

2. Required Capital Calculation

Formula: (Monthly Cost * 12) / (Yield / 100)

We do not account for tax drag in the initial "Required Capital" figure to keep comparisons standardized across different tax brackets.

3. Risk Assessment

Risk levels (Low, Medium, High) are determined by:

  • Dividend History (Years of growth)
  • Payout Ratio (Safe is under 60% for standard stocks, 90% for REITs)
  • Volatility (Beta)
